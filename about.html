ABOUT ME

I am a Front-End Web Developer and UX Designer living Akron, OH. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, Javascript, XML, React and Adobe Creative Suite. I received certificates for all these coding languages and UX, UI best practices at Codecademy and The Interaction Design Foundation, where I am a member. I graduated from high school with a silver math award for the top mathematician in my class. I had a perfect score on my AP calculus exam and was recruited by the CIA my senior year due to my math skills and music abilities. I choose instead to study music, as I was offered multiple full university scholarships. My undergraduate studies were at The University Of Florida, where I graduated Summa Cum Laude. My graduate studies were at Carnegie Mellon, where I was also under full scholarship and was a teachers assistant for Music Theory and Psychology of Music. As a successful musician, I have played on stage with Alan Parsons, Supertramp, Indigo Girls, and Frankie Vali to name a few. Due to my love of all things math related, I changed my career and became a Web Developer once I moved to Ohio. I found that coding and music have a lot in common. They both take a lot of practice and dedication and they are both languages that you have to learn. After two years of study for Web Development and UX Design, I was recently hired by Designhub, an Internationally renown Broadcast Design Agency to develop and redesign their website. I am now looking for full time employment as a Web Developwer / UX/UI designer.
